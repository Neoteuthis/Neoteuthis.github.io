# Welcome to Worldgate

In this game, you are Fateweavers, come together to tell an epic tale and have fun! While the Overseer pulls the strings of reality itself, the rest of you will each be guiding your hero through many strange and wonderful adventures. Many beings in the universe are in touch with one or more of the many sources of magic that influence all things. Your characters are no exception! Most of you will have magical abilities at your disposal to aid your quests. Where will the adventure take you? Far beyond your home province, out of your own continent, and even to other worlds.

Death is not the end for many as well, with stories from a world beyond the mortal coil. Unfortunately, not all souls make it to the afterlife. So, treat your lives as the precious things they are.

In this game, you will Follow clues and go on quests to uncover the secrets of the universe, one lost teddy or errant demon lord at a time. This site will guide you through everything you need to know to get started!
